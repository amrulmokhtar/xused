Marketplace for Waste Items

XUsed is an application for those who want to create monetary value from their waste at home and earn extra income from selling them. It provides a ‘marketplace’ for selected waste items that can be sold to potential buyers or waste collectors. Anyone can sell their used cooking oil, old newspapers and books, tin cans, bottles or glass products, plastics, food waste etc. This app can not only help those in need of extra income but it also helps protect our environment

Sell your waste items with ease

To sell your waste items, just take a photo of the waste items (or just snap them using smartphone) and post those items directly to our platform.

Browse many type of waste items

With XUsed, an interested buyer or waste collector can browse through all the listed items in our platform and select those items accordingly.

How to use XUsed

Seller

  1. Fill up the info about your waste items in the form provided by XUsed
  2. Our XUsed team will review the waste items and notify you when the items have been approved
  3. The waste item will be display in XUsed after approval

Frequently Asked Questions

What is XUsed?

XUsed is an application for people who want to sell their waste and for buyers or waste collectors to buy waste products from potential sellers. The price of a particular waste item is determined by the supply and demand of the item in the market

Why should you use XUsed?

XUsed provide an easy way to access for all types of wastes for sellers to sell their waste items and for buyers or waste collectors to buy those waste items. These items can be easily accessed either through computers or mobile phone

Who can use XUsed?

Anybody. Any individuals (school children, adults, households, groups of people representing NGOs, disabled people, single mothers, companies, factories etc.) can use XUsed to sell their waste

How to attract buyers?

Buyers are always looking at your posts. However, they may want bigger quantity for certain items. Normally, they will come to collect your waste at your place or a centralized collection area depending on negotiations between two parties (i.e., you and the buyers)
