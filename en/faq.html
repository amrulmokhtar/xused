What is XUsed?

XUsed is an application platform which help people to sell their waste items and help buyers to source inventory for their business use

Why should we use XUsed?

XUsed is a marketplace for a variety of waste products for sellers to sell their waste items and for buyers or waste collectors to buy those waste items. These items can be easily accessed either through computers or mobile phone

When does a buyer will come and collect our wastes?

Normally, a buyer will collect your wastes either because they are going through area or they are interested with the quantity that you have

How to attract buyers?

Buyers are always looking at your posting. However, they may want bigger quantity

Why XUsed?

By using XUsed, you do not need to transport your wastes to another place. A buyer will come and collect your waste at your place. If the quantity is small, the buyer may ask you to bring the wastes to the buyer’s place. However, economically it is wise to collect and update your waste quantity until the buyer communicate with you

How do you determine the price for each waste?

The price is determined by supply and demand in the market. These wastes is a valuable commodity which most people ignore or does not know the value

What is the payment term?

At the moment, we are focusing on Cash On Deliver (COD). The buyer will weigh the waste to determine the exact quantity before agreeing on purchasing the waste

Why no one is contacting me or buying my wastes? I have waited for a week

There will be a buyer contacting you in a short period of time. The buyer might not be interested to buy because the quantity is too small and he may want to wait for a larger quantity. XUsed will help to find a buyer the soonest

Does XUsed has a physical store?

Currently, XUsed does act directly as a buyer of waste products. XUsed only displays waste items, i.e., it is a marketplace for waste products. However, there is a plan for XUsed to have its own physical store in the near future

Can I become a buyer?

Yes, you can be a waste collector entrepreneur

Who are the buyers?

The buyers are normally the 3rd party waste collectors around your area

What does the buyers do with the waste?

They will recycle the wastes into a product or they sell to a bigger buyer

Do I need to transport my wastes?

No. You just need to collect and keep it at a safe area. (Eg. Store)

What kind of storage/container do I need, e.g., Used Cooking Oil (UCO)?

For Used Cooking Oil (UCO), you just need to keep in a non-leaking container (e.g., glass or aluminium container)

Do I need to weigh my wastes?

You are encouraged to weigh your wastes before updating in XUsed. However, you can estimate the weight because the buyer will weigh the wastes again to determine the actual quantity

What is the common amount for a buyer to collect from my house?

On average, normally a UCO collector will collect around 10KG’s. However, if the UCO collector is around your neighborhood, he/she will come and collect the UCO along with other

What do I expect to get from my household wastes?

Based on our survey, the average additional income from the household waste is around USD40/RM160 per month. However, the income gain from your household wastes varies on multiple factors

Besides getting additional income, what do I get from XUsed?

Different people have different objectives in life. The common expectation is to gain additional income. However, collecting waste in an efficient manner will also help the government to reduce expenditure in waste management and help mother nature
